## About CoBot

A **CoBot** (collaborative robot) is designed to work alongside humans safely in shared spaces. Unlike traditional industrial robots, CoBots:
- Have built-in force-limiting sensors to prevent injury
- Can be programmed without extensive robotics expertise
- Are used in manufacturing, healthcare, logistics, and education
- Are a key application area for AI-driven automation

In ITAI 3379, students learn to program and operate CoBots as a practical application of AI in physical systems — bridging the gap between software AI and real-world robotics.
