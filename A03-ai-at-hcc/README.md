## Overview

A professional AI-generated educational video titled **"AI at HCC"** created using Synthesia. The video showcases what students in the Artificial Intelligence program at Houston Community College can build and accomplish using modern AI tools — from healthcare AI systems to robotics, machine learning models, and cloud-based solutions.

▶️ [Watch AI at HCC](https://app.synthesia.io/#/video/93a79fb0-b1bd-43cd-a0a7-7794d0f97d40)

---

## Project Activities

### Topic Selection & Concept Planning
We chose "AI at HCC" because it reflects the innovative work happening in the college's AI program and aligns with the goals of the course. The goal was to create a video that both informs and inspires students about the possibilities of AI.

### Scriptwriting & Content Development
The team collaborated to write a concise script explaining the types of AI projects HCC students can create, focusing on clarity, accuracy, and real-world relevance. The script was refined several times to ensure the avatar delivered the message naturally.

### Avatar & Template Selection
A professional Synthesia avatar was selected to maintain a polished and academic tone, paired with a template that matched the theme of technology and education.

### Video Production
Script uploaded, timing adjusted, voice style selected, and visual elements added. Pacing was carefully managed for smooth transitions throughout.

### Review & Final Editing
Multiple drafts reviewed with feedback rounds to adjust pronunciation, timing, and visual layout.
